01 - Introduction

About this policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to Arrow Escape (the "App"), a free mobile game developed and published by Funzar Labs ("we," "us," "our") on the Apple App Store. It explains what information we collect when you use the App, what we do with it, and the choices you have.

Arrow Escape is a casual puzzle game rated 4+ on the App Store. The App is free to download and play, supported by advertising. There are no in-app purchases.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Quick summary

Arrow Escape collects only what we need to run the game, fix bugs, and show ads. We don't sell your personal data. We don't ask for your name, email, or location. You can disable ad personalization any time in iOS Settings.

02 - App Privacy

Apple App Privacy summary.

The following types of data may be collected and linked to your identity for the purposes listed. This matches the Privacy Nutrition Label shown on our App Store listing.

Identifiers
Device ID / advertising identifier (IDFA) - used for analytics, ad measurement, and showing relevant ads (only with your consent via ATT).
Usage Data
Product interaction, gameplay events, levels played, ad interactions - used for analytics and product improvement.
Diagnostics
Crash logs, performance data, device model, OS version - used to fix bugs and improve stability.

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, physical address, photos, contacts, health data, financial information, or precise location. The App does not offer in-app purchases.

03 - Information We Collect

What we collect.

Automatically collected

  • Device identifiers - including IDFV (Identifier for Vendors) and, if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission, IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers).
  • Device & system information - device model, iOS version, language, region, screen size, and time zone.
  • Gameplay data - levels played, scores, in-game progress, session length, and feature usage.
  • Crash & performance data - anonymous crash logs and performance metrics so we can fix issues.
  • Ad interaction data - which ads were shown, viewed, or clicked. Used for ad measurement and to avoid showing the same ad repeatedly.

We do not collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your precise GPS location.
  • Your contacts, photos, calendar, or microphone data.
  • Health, fitness, biometric, or sensitive personal information.
  • Payment information - the App has no in-app purchases.
04 - How We Use It

How we use your information.

We use the data we collect to:

  • Operate the App and save your game progress on your device.
  • Diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Understand how the App is used so we can improve features, level design, and balance.
  • Show ads inside the App and measure their effectiveness.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
05 - Third Parties

Third-party services we use.

Arrow Escape uses the following third-party services. Each handles your data under its own privacy policy:

Apple App Store

Apple processes downloads and updates. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand gameplay patterns and Firebase Crashlytics to capture anonymous crash reports. Firebase may collect device identifiers, IP address (used to derive approximate country only and then discarded), and app usage events. See Firebase Privacy & Security and Google Privacy Policy.

CleverAdsSolutions (CAS.AI)

We use CAS as our ad mediation platform. CAS shows ads from multiple ad networks (such as Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network, Unity Ads, AppLovin, and others) and may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data. See the CAS Privacy Policy and the policies of the underlying ad networks.

Apple AdAttributionKit

We use Apple's privacy-preserving AdAttributionKit framework to measure ad campaign performance without tracking individual users. See Apple Ad Attribution.

These providers may use SDKs and device identifiers solely to perform the services we engage them for. We do not sell your personal data to any of them.

06 - Tracking

App Tracking Transparency.

The first time you open Arrow Escape, iOS will ask whether you allow the App to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites. This is called App Tracking Transparency (ATT).

  • If you allow tracking: we and our ad partners can use your IDFA to show you more relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • If you don't allow tracking: you'll still see ads, but they won't be personalized. The game works exactly the same.

You can change your choice any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or in Settings → Arrow Escape → Allow Tracking.

07 - Advertising

How advertising works.

Arrow Escape is free to play. To support development, we show ads inside the App. These may include:

  • Banner ads - a small ad shown at the top or bottom of certain screens.
  • Interstitial ads - a full-screen ad shown between levels or at natural break points.
  • Rewarded ads - optional ads you can choose to watch in exchange for in-game rewards (such as extra lives or coins).

Ads are served through CleverAdsSolutions (CAS.AI), which mediates between multiple ad networks. Ad content is controlled by these networks, not by Funzar Labs. We work to keep ads age-appropriate for our 4+ rating, but cannot guarantee every ad shown will match every player's preferences.

If you see an inappropriate ad, please report it to us at hello@funzarlabs.com with a screenshot if possible - we'll investigate and work with our ad partners to block similar ads.

Disable personalized ads

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time by:

  • Declining or revoking ATT permission (see Section 06).
  • Going to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising and turning off Personalized Ads.

You will still see ads, but they will be non-personalized.

08 - Children

Children's privacy.

Arrow Escape is rated 4+ on the App Store. It is a casual puzzle game that may be enjoyed by children and players of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously and design the App to be kid-friendly:

  • No in-app purchases - children cannot accidentally spend money in the App.
  • No social features - there are no chat, messaging, or user-generated content features.
  • No personal data collection - we do not ask for names, emails, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • Age-appropriate ads - we work with our ad provider (CAS) to ensure ads shown are appropriate for a 4+ audience.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) without verifiable parental consent, as required by the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and similar laws elsewhere.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@funzarlabs.com and we will delete it promptly.

Parental controls

iOS includes powerful parental controls through Screen Time and Family Sharing. We recommend using these features to manage your child's app usage. You can also restrict in-app advertising for children's accounts.

09 - Your Rights

Your rights & choices.

You have the right to:

  • Access - request information about what data we hold related to your device.
  • Delete - request deletion of data linked to your device.
  • Opt out of personalized ads - disable App Tracking Transparency permission in iOS Settings.
  • Reset advertising identifier - go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → View Ad Targeting Information.
  • Delete the App - uninstalling the App stops further data collection from your device.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@funzarlabs.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

For users in the EU / UK (GDPR)

You have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to data portability, restriction of processing, objection to processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal basis for processing is your consent (for advertising and personalized ads) and our legitimate interest (for analytics, crash reporting, and fraud prevention).

For users in California (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, and to opt out of any sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law. However, the sharing of device identifiers for advertising purposes may be considered "sharing" under CPRA - you can opt out by disabling ATT permission as described above.

10 - Security

How we protect your data.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encrypted connections (HTTPS / TLS) for all network requests, restricted access to production systems, and regular security reviews of our SDKs and dependencies.

That said, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If we discover a security incident that affects your data, we will notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

11 - Retention

How long we keep data.

  • Gameplay & analytics data - retained by Firebase for up to 14 months, then automatically deleted or anonymized.
  • Crash reports - retained for up to 90 days, then deleted.
  • Ad interaction data - retained by CAS and underlying ad networks according to their own retention policies (typically 90-180 days).
  • Device identifiers - deleted when you uninstall the App or reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings.
12 - International

Funzar Labs is based in Pakistan. Our third-party service providers (Apple, Google Firebase, CAS) operate globally and may process your information in countries other than your own, including the United States and the European Union.

By using the App, you acknowledge that your information may be transferred and processed in these jurisdictions, which may have data protection laws different from those in your country. We use providers that maintain industry-standard safeguards and, where required, standard contractual clauses to protect your data.

When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you within the App or by updating the App Store listing.
