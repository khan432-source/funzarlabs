Privacy Policy

MELON RUSH
Last updated: 19 May 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Melon Rush ("the App", "we", "us"), published by Funzar Labs, handles information when you use the App. By installing or using Melon Rush, you agree to the practices described below.

1 Information We Collect

Melon Rush is designed to be privacy-friendly. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, or contact details (a nickname you optionally type in stays only on your device).

The App may involve the following data:

  • Game progress & settings - your level progress, scores, high scores, coins, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to us.
  • Advertising data - see Section 3. The App may display ads in a future update. When ads are active, the ad provider may process device identifiers and ad interaction data.
  • Diagnostic data - basic crash and performance information may be collected to help us fix problems and improve the App.

The App does not currently offer in-app purchases and does not collect or process any payment information.

2 How We Use Information

  • To save and restore your game progress on your device.
  • To display and measure advertising (when the ad feature is enabled - see Section 3).
  • To diagnose crashes and improve App stability and performance.

3 Advertising

Advertising may be introduced to the App in a future update. When ads become active, the App will use CAS.AI as its advertising mediation provider. CAS.AI works with third-party ad networks that may collect and process data under their own privacy policies, such as device advertising identifiers and ad interaction data.

Because the App is available to a mixed audience that may include children, ads shown to users in age groups that include children will use non-personalized, child-directed treatment where required by Google Play and applicable law.

You can limit ad personalization at any time through your device settings (for example, "Reset advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android). For details on CAS.AI's data practices, see https://cas.ai/privacy-policy/.

This section describes ads as a planned feature. If your published build does not yet contain ads, that is consistent with the wording above. Once ads are live, no further change to this policy is required, but you should re-confirm the Google Play Data Safety form reflects ad data collection.

4 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Information is shared only with the service providers needed to operate the App:

  • Google Play - for app distribution.
  • CAS.AI and its ad network partners - for advertising, once the ad feature is enabled.

5 Children's Privacy

Melon Rush is suitable for a mixed, family audience and may be used by children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not require an account and do not knowingly collect personal information (such as real name, email, or precise location) from any user, including children.
  • Game progress is stored only on the device.
  • When advertising is enabled, ads served to audiences that include children will be non-personalized and configured for child-directed treatment, in line with the Google Play Families policy and applicable laws such as COPPA and GDPR.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details in Section 9 and we will take steps to delete it.

6 Data Retention & Deletion

Game progress is stored locally on your device. You can erase all of it at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the App. Any data processed by CAS.AI (once ads are enabled) or Google is retained according to their own policies.

7 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time - for example, when the advertising feature goes live. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
