About the Game

Arrow Escape is a relaxing yet brain-twisting slide puzzle with one goal: free the trapped pet. Tap an arrow and it slides until something blocks it. Move the right arrows in the right order, open the cage, and watch the pet escape to freedom. Hundreds of handcrafted levels across five difficulty tiers keep you challenged.

Easy through Master. Always challenged.
