02 - FAQ

Common questions.

The questions we get most often. Tap any to expand.

What games does Funzar Labs make?

Funzar Labs is a mobile game studio building casual games for iOS and Android. We are just getting started, with our first titles rolling out now. Follow us on Instagram or join the waitlist on our homepage to see what is next.

Are your games free to play?

Yes. Our games are free to download and play. They are supported by advertising. Where a game includes optional in-app purchases, that will always be clearly stated on its App Store and Google Play listing.

What platforms are your games on?

Our games are built for mobile and are available on:

  • iOS via the Apple App Store (iPhone & iPad)
  • Android via Google Play

Availability can vary by title. Check the specific game listing for details.

I lost my game progress. Can you help?

Email us at hello@funzarlabs.com with:

  • The name of the game
  • Your device model and iOS / Android version
  • Approximate level or progress you were on
  • When you noticed the issue

We will do our best to recover what we can.

How do I stop seeing personalized ads?

You can opt out of personalized ads any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → turn off for the game
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → turn on "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

You will still see ads, but they will not be personalized. The game works exactly the same.

I saw an inappropriate ad. What do I do?

We work hard to keep ads age-appropriate, but with the volume of ads served by our ad networks we cannot review every single one. Please email hello@funzarlabs.com with the game name and a screenshot if possible, and we will work with our ad partners to block similar ads.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Uninstalling a game stops further data collection. To request deletion of any data we already have, email us at hello@funzarlabs.com with the game name, and we will process it within 30 days. Each game has its own privacy policy linked on its store listing.

Are you hiring?

We are a small studio just getting started. We are not actively hiring right now, but if you are talented and excited about what we are building, send us a portfolio and a quick note at hello@funzarlabs.com. We keep great people on file.

How can I follow updates?

The two best ways:

  • Follow us on Instagram for behind-the-scenes content and new game announcements
  • Join the email waitlist on our homepage for launch alerts
