01 — Introduction

The short version.

Funzar Labs ("we," "us," "our") makes mobile games and runs the website at funzarlabs.com. We respect your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you use our website or play our games, what we do with it, and the choices you have.

In plain English

We collect the minimum we need to run our services and improve our games. We never sell your data. You can email us anytime to ask what we have, change it, or delete it.

02 — Information We Collect

What we collect.

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

From the website

  • Email address — only if you sign up for our waitlist or contact us. We use this to notify you when we launch and to reply to your messages.
  • Basic device & browser information — your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages viewed. This is collected automatically through standard web logs and helps us keep the site secure and understand traffic patterns.

From our mobile games

  • Device identifiers — such as your device's advertising ID, model, OS version, and language. These help us deliver the right build, fix bugs, and (where applicable) show ads.
  • Gameplay data — levels played, in-game progress, scores, settings, and crash reports. This is used to improve gameplay, sync your progress across devices, and diagnose issues.
  • Purchase information — if you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We receive a confirmation that the purchase happened, but we never see or store your payment card details.
  • Optional account data — if you choose to sign in with a third-party service (e.g. Apple, Google, Facebook) or create an account, we receive the information you authorize that service to share.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information (such as health, biometric, or precise location data) and we don't ask for it.

03 — How We Use It

How we use your information.

We use the data we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our website and games.
  • Send you launch announcements, updates, and replies to your messages (and only that — no marketing fluff).
  • Sync your game progress and personalize your experience.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, cheating, abuse, or technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We will not use your information for purposes incompatible with the ones described here without telling you first.

04 — Sharing

Who we share with.

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only in the following situations:

  • Service providers we trust to help us operate (for example, our email service, cloud hosting, analytics, crash reporting, and customer support tools). These providers can only use your information to do work for us.
  • App stores & payment processors — Apple and Google handle the actual purchase transactions and apply their own privacy policies.
  • Advertising partners — if a game shows ads, our advertising SDKs may receive a device-level identifier to serve relevant ads. You can limit this in your device settings ("Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Reset advertising ID" on Android).
  • Legal requests — if we are required to share information by valid law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Funzar Labs or others.
  • Business transfers — if Funzar Labs is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your information may be part of that transfer. We will notify you before that happens.
05 — Cookies

Cookies & analytics.

Our website uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies to make the site work, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use it. We may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Plausible, Fathom, or similar) and standard advertising attribution tools.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Most parts of the site will continue to work without them.

06 — Children

Children's privacy.

Our games may be enjoyed by players of all ages, but our services are not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below this age without verifiable parental consent.

If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at hello@funzarlabs.com and we will delete it promptly.

07 — Your Rights

Your rights & choices.

You have the right to:

  • Access — ask us what personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct — ask us to update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete — ask us to delete your information (some data may be kept for legal or security reasons).
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of waitlist or marketing emails any time, using the link in the email or by replying to it.
  • Object & restrict — limit how we use certain data, where the law allows.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@funzarlabs.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days).

08 — Security

How we protect your data.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. This includes encryption in transit (HTTPS), restricted access to production systems, and regular security reviews.

That said, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. If we learn of a security breach affecting your data, we'll notify you and the relevant authorities as required by law.

09 — Changes

Changes to this policy.

We may update this policy occasionally — for example, when we launch a new product or when laws change. When we make material changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you by email or through the app.

Your continued use of our website or games after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

If anything in this policy is unclear, or if you'd like to exercise any of your rights, we'd love to hear from you.
